Cyber Puzzles & Brain Teasers

1. Cyber Maze

  • Guide a digital avatar through a maze avoiding cyber threats.
  • Use printed maze worksheets or online generators.
  • Each blocked path represents a cyber threat (phishing, malware).

2. Spot the Scam

  • Give a list of emails/messages, students identify which are scams.
  • Can be done on paper or a screen.

3. Secure or Not?

  • Match website screenshots to secure/insecure labels.
  • Great for learning about HTTPS and lock icons.

4. Decode the Secret

  • Use simple ciphers (Caesar, binary) to hide messages.
  • Let kids decode and learn encryption basics.

5. Two Truths and a Lie (Cyber Edition)

  • Give 3 statements about cyber safety.
  • Students guess the false one and explain why.
